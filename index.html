DropSweat Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2025

DropSweat does not sell your data, does not show ads, and does not share your information with third parties. Your fitness data is yours.

1. Overview

DropSweat ("we", "our", or "the app") is a fitness tracking application that helps you log workouts, monitor nutrition, and track your progress over time. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that data.

By using DropSweat, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. Data We Collect

We collect only what is necessary to provide the app's features:

  • Account information — your email address, used to create and authenticate your account.
  • Profile data — name, age, gender, height, weight, fitness goals, and equipment preferences you provide during setup.
  • Workout data — exercises, sets, reps, weights, dates, and notes you log.
  • Nutrition data — calorie and macro entries you log manually.
  • Weight history — body weight entries you record over time.
  • App preferences — theme, unit system (lbs/kg), notification settings, and other in-app configuration.

We do not collect location data, contacts, camera or microphone access, or any data from other apps on your device.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To display your personal dashboard, stats, and progress charts within the app.
  • To calculate a daily calorie recommendation based on your profile information.
  • To send local workout reminder notifications at times you configure — these are scheduled entirely on your device.
  • To remember your preferences between sessions.

We do not use your data for advertising, analytics platforms, or machine learning training.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a cloud database provider. All data is:

  • Encrypted in transit using TLS (HTTPS).
  • Encrypted at rest on Supabase's infrastructure.
  • Protected by row-level security policies — your data is only accessible to your own authenticated account.

No other users or third parties can access your data.

5. Notifications

DropSweat uses local notifications only. Workout reminders are scheduled on your device and are not sent from a server. No notification content is transmitted over the internet. You can disable notifications at any time in your device's Settings app.

6. Third-Party Services

DropSweat uses the following third-party service:

  • Supabase — database and authentication provider. Supabase Privacy Policy.

We do not integrate with advertising networks, social media platforms, or analytics services.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data — all data you've entered is visible within the app.
  • Delete your data — contact us to permanently delete your account and all associated data.
  • Export your data — contact us to request a copy of your data.
  • Correct your data — you can update your profile and entries directly in the app at any time.

8. Children's Privacy

DropSweat is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of DropSweat after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact

If you have any questions, requests, or concerns about this Privacy Policy or your data, please contact us at the support email listed on the app's store page.
